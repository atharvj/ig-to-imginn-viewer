# GreasyFork Description

View public Instagram profiles without logging in.

This userscript redirects public Instagram profile, post, and reel links to Imginn, where you can browse profile grids and open posts in a popup without losing your place. It is designed for logged-out browsing when Instagram pushes public profile pages toward a login screen.

Features:

- View public Instagram profiles without being logged in.
- Redirect public Instagram profiles, posts, and reels to Imginn.
- Open Imginn posts and reels in a popup on the profile page.
- Keep your place in the profile grid while viewing posts.
- Clean up common Imginn spacing, share rows, and popup header clutter.

Limitations:

- Public profiles only.
- Depends on what Imginn exposes publicly.
- Does not unlock private accounts, restricted content, or posts that Imginn returns as `Content Not Found`.
